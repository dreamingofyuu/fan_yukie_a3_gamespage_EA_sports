Reason: I only play The Sims when I have time to. This includes time that I don't have academic work or when none of my friends are online to play. I don't remember how much I've spent on expansion packs and dlcs for this game, but it's been quite a few. I first got this game for my love of interior design. When I got this game, I was so sure that I was going to go to Ryerson for Interior Design, but I ended up going elsewhere. Nonetheless, it's always fun to customize my OCs from books I write into The Sims. I am able to build and better visualize how my imaginary world and characters would look. As for the amount I spent on this game... as much as I'd like to disclose that information, for my personal santity, I wish to not. To put it in perspective, Sims isn't very fun unless there are copious amounts of CC (custom content) or DLCs. Most CC are free to download from the internet, but the DLCs come directly from the game. Albeit I have gotten some 'stuff packs' for free, I still have a couple expansion packs to play with my game.
